7 months to go....!

* Continue taking prenatal vitamins: Prenatal vitamins are important throughout your pregnancy, so make sure you continue taking them.

* Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water and avoid sugary drinks and caffeine.

* Eat a healthy diet: Eat plenty of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins. Avoid processed foods, sugary drinks, and foods high in saturated fats.

*Exercise: Moderate exercise is safe during pregnancy and can help you feel better. Talk to your healthcare provider about what exercises are safe for you to do.


* Schedule your first prenatal appointment:Schedule your first prenatal appointment with your healthcare provider. They can answer any questions you may have and monitor your pregnancy.


* Manage morning sickness: Try eating small, frequent meals throughout the day, and avoid foods that trigger nausea.

* As your breasts show symptoms of heaviness, begin using supportive bras to prevent sagging later.

* Get plenty of rest: Pregnancy can be tiring, so make sure you're getting plenty of rest. Try to aim for 7-8 hours of sleep each night.

* Avoid certain foods: Continue to avoid raw or undercooked meat, fish, and eggs, and unpasteurized dairy products.

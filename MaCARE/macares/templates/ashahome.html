ASHA Worker Home Page

Accredited Social Health Activist, or ASHA, worker is female healthcare activist trained to facilitate the work of the nationwide public health system within their community. ASHA workers are a core part of the National Rural Health Mission launched by the Government of India. ASHA workers are trained to act as a care provider at the community level and facilitate access to healthcare, medicine and sanitation services. ASHA workers also work to raise the level of awareness of health issues among the marginalised sections within the community.
